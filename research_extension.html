RESEARCH EXTENSION

From segmentation results to evaluation reliability

The research extension organizes follow-up evaluation ideas around output stability, boundary behavior, and hardware-aware comparison. It is not presented as a separate finished paper, but as a structured direction for deeper analysis.

  • Why output stability is useful after segmentation result review.

Current public scope

The extension pages document evaluation directions that can be expanded later with additional experiments. They are included to show research planning and review structure, not to present a completed benchmark.
